## Brief Explanation of What Each Certification Represents

### CompTIA Security+
The CompTIA Security+ certification validates foundational cybersecurity knowledge and practical security skills commonly required for entry-level cybersecurity positions. The certification covers topics such as network security, system hardening, identity management, cryptography, and security best practices.

### CompTIA CySA+
The CompTIA CySA+ certification focuses on defensive cybersecurity operations and analytical skills used by cybersecurity analysts. It emphasizes threat detection, security monitoring, vulnerability assessment, incident response, and the use of SIEM technologies in operational environments.
